About the campaign

Join schools, NGOs and municipalities as we clean riverbanks, collect citizen-science samples and map pollution hotspots across the Lower Danube. Every participating group contributes data that feeds directly into the public dashboard.

How to participate

  1. Sign up and pick a locality near you.
  2. Download the field-kit checklist and watch the 5-minute tutorial.
  3. Run a cleanup + sampling event and upload your photos and data.
  4. Earn the River Guardian badge and a digital certificate.
