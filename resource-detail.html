Danube Wetland Atlas 2025

Published April 2026 · SoS2LearnDBS consortium

About this resource

The Danube Wetland Atlas 2025 maps 230+ restoration-relevant wetland sites across 9 countries, with priority scores, biodiversity indicators and current management status. The atlas supports community groups, researchers and policymakers in targeting Nature-based Solutions where they will have the greatest impact.

What's inside

  • Site inventory with GIS coordinates.
  • Priority scoring methodology.
  • Restoration opportunity maps by country.
  • Case studies from the Lower Danube and the Delta.
